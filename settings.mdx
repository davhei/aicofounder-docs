Settings is where you manage your profile, email preferences, data preferences, and billing.

To open settings, click **Settings** in the dashboard sidebar.

<img src="/images/settings-profile.webp" alt="Settings profile tab with email, name, and timezone fields" className="w-full rounded-lg" style={{border: '1px solid #b3b3b3'}} />

## My profile

Update your account details:

- **Email** - Your login email address
- **Preferred name** - Displayed on the dashboard
- **Password** - Your login password
- **Timezone** - Used for scheduling and timestamps

## Communication

Control which emails you receive:

- **Onboarding emails** - Tips and guidance to help you get started
- **Weekly newsletter** - Weekly insights and founder stories
- **Announcements** - Product updates and new features

Each preference can be toggled on or off independently.

<img src="/images/settings-communication-preferences.webp" alt="Communication tab showing email preference toggles" className="w-full rounded-lg" style={{border: '1px solid #b3b3b3'}} />

## Data preferences

Enable or disable privacy mode. When privacy mode is on, your project data is not reviewed internally. See [Privacy mode](/privacy-mode) for details.

## Affiliate

Join the [affiliate program](https://aicofounder.com/affiliate) to earn 20% commission for the first 6 months on customers you refer.

## Plan & billing

View your current plan and upgrade if you want access to more features.

Subscribers can click **Billing portal** to open Stripe, where you can view receipts, update your payment method, and manage your subscription.
