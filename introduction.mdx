# Welcome to AI Cofounder

AI Cofounder helps you build and launch your projects with AI. This documentation covers everything you need to know to get the most out of the platform.

## What is AI Cofounder?

AI Cofounder is your AI-powered partner for building and launching digital products. From ideation to deployment, it guides you through every step of the process.

## Getting Started

Get up and running with AI Cofounder in just a few steps:

1. **Create an account** at [aicofounder.com](https://aicofounder.com/signup)
2. **Start a new project** from your dashboard
3. **Describe your idea** and let AI Cofounder help you build it
